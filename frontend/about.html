About FloodWatch

FloodWatch is a community-driven platform dedicated to providing timely and accurate flood-risk information to keep people safe. Our goal is to leverage both official data and crowdsourced, on-the-ground reports to create a comprehensive and actionable alerting system.

We believe that by empowering communities with shared knowledge, we can help save lives, protect property, and build more resilient neighborhoods in the face of natural disasters.
